BACKGROUND


" The New York City Taxi & Limousine Commission has released a staggeringly detailed historical dataset covering over 1.1 billion individual taxi trips (i.e. Green Taxi and Yellow Taxi) and for-hired vehicles trips (i.e. Uber and Lyft) in the city since 2014. Taken as a whole, the detailed trip-level data is more than just a vast list of taxi pick-up and drop-off coordinates: Itâ€™s a story of New York.

We compare the operation status and spatial-temporal distribution of different vehicle services in NYC to answer the research questions:
- Which service do New Yorkers prefer to choose?
- Is there any business dominated regions for different type of vehicles?
- Is there any differences of demands in different hour of day or different day of week?
This website will address the questions above and explore more. "

TAXI / YELLOW

/ Service scale

Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens and Staten Island

/ Pricing Method

Yellow Taxi is the most common typle of taxi in NYC, which can pick up and drop off riders in each borough as well as Airports. Taxis do not have surge pricing, but riders might need to wait longer when demand exceeds supply. Taxis do, however, add a $0.50 surcharge in the evening (8:00 p.m. to 6:00 a.m.) and a $1 surcharge during rush hour (4:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.) from Monday to Friday.

TAXI / GREEN

/ Service scale

Outer Borough (Bronx, Brooklyn, Queens and Staten Island) and North part of Manhattan

/ Pricing Method

Green Taxi is called "Boro taxi" in NYC, which is allowed to pick up passengers (street hails or calls) in outer boroughs (excluding John F. Kennedy International Airport and La Guardia Airport unless arranged in advance) and in Manhattan above East 96th and West 110th Streets. This type of taxi was launched to fill the gap in outer borough.

Its service price is the same as Yellow Taxi.

FHV / UBER

/ Service scale

Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens and Staten Island

/ Pricing Method

Uber, as one of the companies of For-hired Vihecles, is only accessible through an up-to-date smartphone. Since passengers request all Uber rides through the app, there is no waiting on the street or suffering from bad weather. The app will let users know when driver arrives.
